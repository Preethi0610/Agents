# Banoffee Pie Recipe

A classic British dessert with a sweet-salty graham cracker crust filled with dulce de leche, banana slices, and topped with whipped cream and chocolate shavings.

## Ingredients

- Graham cracker crust:
  - 1-1/4 cups graham cracker crumbs
  - 1/2 cup butter, melted
  - 1/3 cup sugar
  - 1/4 teaspoon salt
- Filling:
  - 3 bananas, sliced 1/4 inch thick
  - 1 cup dulce de leche
  - 1-1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
  - 1-1/2 teaspoons instant espresso powder
  - 3 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
  - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  - Chocolate shavings (optional)

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 3506F. In a large bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, sugar, and salt. Press into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch deep-dish pie plate. Bake about 15 minutes until set. Cool completely.
2. Place heavy whipping cream, espresso powder, confectioners9 sugar, and vanilla extract in a large bowl. Whisk until soft peaks form. Set aside.
3. Spread dulce de leche into cooled crust.
4. Top with two layers of sliced bananas.
5. Spoon whipped cream over the bananas.
6. Refrigerate at least 3 hours or overnight.
7. Garnish with chocolate shavings before serving, if desired.

## Tips

- If dulce de leche is stiff, warm your spatula under hot water before spreading.
- Use firm but ripe bananas for best flavor and texture.
- To prevent bananas from browning if pre-cut, squeeze fresh lemon juice on them.
- You can make the pie a day ahead and store it covered in the fridge.

Enjoy your delicious and easy Banoffee Pie!
